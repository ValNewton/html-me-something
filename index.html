I'm glad I marched

I couldn't make it to Washington for the march, but my daughter & I joined thousands of others at the Woman's March on St. Louis. I was hoping for a good turn out, and St. Louis did not dissapoint. Police estimates put the attendance between 11,000 & 13,000.

Great Signs
Great Speakers
Senator Claire McCaskill gave a short impromptu speech. She is circled in pink in the photo. The rally was held east of the Old Courthouse at the new park connecting downtown to the Arch.

Nationwide attendance is HUGE!

Current estimates say a minimum of 3.7 million people participated in the Woman's March on Washington or sister marches accross the nation. Two professors are collecting the data and spreadsheet is available online. 548 U.S marches are listed. You can read more at Quartz
